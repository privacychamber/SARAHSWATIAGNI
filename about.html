The Woman Behind the Craft

Artist, Healer, Guide, Spirit

A Vision of Transformation

Merging Artistry with Inner Alignment

"I believe that our outer form and our inner state are deeply interconnected. When we work on our crown, we are locking in intentions. When we breathe, we are releasing the past."

For over a decade, Sarah Agni has been navigating the intersection of physical styling and energetic alignment. Rooted in India and drawing inspiration from global tribal cultures, Bali wellness practices, and the transformational energy of Burning Man, she has cultivated a unique personal brand.

Her journey began with a deep appreciation for dreadlocks as natural sculptures. Over the years, this visual craft evolved. She realized that sitting for hours in a dreadlock chair is a deeply intimate, meditative process. Clients began sharing stories, crying, laughing, and releasing. This realization prompted her to pursue clinical hypnotherapy, breathwork training, and somatic release practices to support her clients holistically.

10+ Years of Healing Guidance

Somatic Integration & Energy Release

Believing that the body retains memory of trauma and emotional tension, Sarah trained in circular somatic breathing, breathwork mechanics, and spiritual hypnotherapy. She hosts private healing retreats in Bali and India, creating safe spaces for people to heal anxiety, somatic tension, and emotional blocks.

By combining dreadlock artistry with these energetic therapies, she offers a profound physical-spiritual rebirth. She facilitates emotional block release, helping people feel lighter, more grounded, and connected to their truth.

Integrity First

Safety, consent, and scientific clarity back every breathwork and hypnotherapy session she holds.

Natural Precision

No waxes or chemical shortcuts are used in dreadlocks. Only natural organic hook artistry.

Igniting the External Flame

The Discipline of Fire Dance

Fire dancing is Sarah's active meditation. For her, fire is a powerful cleansing force. It requires total presence, coordination, and respect. It taught her how to transmute fear into focus.

Through fire performance workshops and staff/poi lessons, she teaches people how to move with confidence, handle fear, and connect to their primal energy safely. It's a journey of physical calibration, flow state activation, and trust.

"When you dance with fire, you cannot think about the past or future. You must exist completely in the present second."
